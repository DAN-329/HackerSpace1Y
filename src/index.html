Welcome To The PESU GUIDE


The PESU Guide is website where anyone can find more information about different places and facilities in PES University both on the RR and EC campuses.


This site was created by the HackerSpace club of PES university for Hacktoberfest 2020. Everyone is encouraged to add more content and changes ranging from the styling to the information provided on this webpage.
